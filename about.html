About Mana


Vision and Mission:

According to UNICEF, hunger and malnutrition are the biggest risks to child health worldwide - killing more than TB, malaria and AIDS combined. RUTF cures kids suffering from severe acute malnutrition. MANA produces RUTF and partners such as UNICEF get it the last mile to kids in need. We have had an amazing success, going from start up in 2011 to profitability. We have made enough RUTF to treat 350,000 kids! We've established ourseleves as a global supplier... but it's not enough. Our end goal is simple but audacious: To lead the charge to end malnutrition.

Context:

MANA must do more than tap the food aid marketplace or shift business from competitors to scale into a force that could end malnutrition. We must expand funding for RUTF and create a new marketplace. Here is how we plan to do it: One of our biggest challenges in America is we have too many calories. What if we could monetize these calories and trade them? If colories could be collected and monetized we could transfer them to kids in developing countries who need them.
