Mana is leading the charge to end malnutrition.

Mana provides ready-to-use food (RTUF) to cure children from severe acute malnutrition. Currently Mana has produced RUTF to treat 350,000 children. But, Mana is looking to expand funding for RUTF to create a new marketplace.

You can help by tracking the calories you burn.

By working toward a healthier and happier lifestyle, you can actually help provide for a a child's life. 30 minutes on a treadmill equals roughly one 500 calorie packet, which is enough to nourish a child for a day.

And your team can compete against other teams at your workplace.

Each week your team will earn extra "points" or calories for completing certain challenges together. Your team can also earn badges, organize events, and wager points to challenge other teams.
